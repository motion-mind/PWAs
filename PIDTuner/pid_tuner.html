PPCL Control Loop Calculator

Calculates Proportional Gain (pg) and starting Integral Gain (ig) based on the control range and throttling range.

Max Output - Min Output. e.g., 100.0 (for 0-100%) or 5.0 (for 3-8 psi).

Desired error for 100% output change. Smaller TR = higher, more sensitive gain (pg).

Calculated Initial Gains:

Proportional Gain (pg): 0
Initial Integral Gain (ig): 0

*Formula: pg = (Output Span / TR) × 1000. ig = pg × 0.02 (Recommended start).

Note: Derivative gain (dg) is assumed 0. Final tuning is manual after pg and ig are set.
