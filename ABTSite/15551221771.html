Copying one day's activities

  • While in Edit mode, click Copy in the heading.

①

Select the day you want to copy
This text guides you through the copy process.

②

Next button
Click Next, and select the target day(s) for your copy.

③

Paste button
Click Paste.
Changes are automatically saved, and the copied day overwrites the day(s) it is pasted to.
