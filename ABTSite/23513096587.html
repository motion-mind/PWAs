Setting the certificate from internal to external

  1. Go to Building.
  2. Open the BACnet/SC task.
  3. In the Building structure tab, select the device.
  4. In the Details pane of the device, select BACnet/SC.
  5. In the Certificate authority drop-down list, select External.
