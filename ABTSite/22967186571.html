Onboarding checklist

The onboarding checklist provides an overview of all onboarding processes for all Building X apps. The instructions in Accounts, Devices and Data Setup are generally valid and must be performed. A distinction is made between mandatory, recommended, and optional steps. For each business app a chapter with a checklist and additional steps is available in the section Business apps. Make sure to consult the checklist for your apps and complete all the necessary steps.
