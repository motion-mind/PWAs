Importing for ABT Site an alternative BACnet/SC operational certificate

  1. You have an alternative root certificate e.g. from the IT department.
  1. Go to Settings.
  2. Open the Certificates task.
  3. Select the BACnet/SC certificates tab.
  4. In the Details view, click Import signed ABT operational certificate.
  5. Select the root certificate, and click Open.
  6. You have imported a root certificate for the ABT Site. The old root certificate is still present in the project for a transition time until you download to all devices.
