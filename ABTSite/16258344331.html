Unlocking from Pack & Go

After creating a Pack & Go file the building hierarchy, the device and the corresponding appplication template are locked in the original project. The original project is the project from where the Pack & Go file was created.

Restriction on locked devices and templates (inside original project)

  • The template configuration cannot be changed
  • Instance parameters cannot be changed
  • Instance properties (naming, BACnet IP / BACnet MS/TP etc.) cannot be changed

 

Unlock from Pack & Go

  1. At least one device is part of a Pack and Go package. The status field of the device is Pack & Go.
  1. Select a locked device or a locked building hierarchy or a locked template.
  2. Right-click and select Unlock from Pack & Go.
  3. Click OK.
  1. The device is unlocked.
  2. The status field of the device is set from Pack & Go to Modified.
  3. All other not selected devices that are part of the locked template are also unlocked.
  4. The building hierarchy is unlocked if no other devices in that building hierarchy are locked, otherwise the building hierarchy remains locked.
