Main functions

Main components

  • Collect events and alarm indications from the automation station and acknowledge all events (by reading or writing from or to AS_VIEW).
  • Distribute acknowledge information to the plants in their own automation station.
  • Suppress event notifications.
  • Logic for WLAN activation
