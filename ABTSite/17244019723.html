Upgrading ABT Site and ABT Pro

You can upgrade the ABT that is installed on your machine.

Upgrading existing ABT (Version 5.2)

You cannot upgrade ABT Site and ABT Pro from V5.2 to V6.0. You must uninstall V5.2 and install V6.0.

Upgrading ABT to V6.0 from V4.4 and below:

Upgrade from ABT V4.x to V6.0 is not supported. To use V6.0, you must first uninstall the lower version for example, V4.4 and then install V6.0 by using the ABT Installer.
