Front module display for AQR2570 and AQR2576 sensors

  1. You want to view the front module display of the AQR2570 and AQR2576 sensors.
  1. Go to Building > Building structure.
  2. Right-click a configured device and select Go to engineering.
  3. Open the KNX PL-Link task.
  4. Open the Library tab and expand Field devices > KNX PL-Link folder.
  5. Drag the AQR2570 and AQR2576 sensors and drop them to the network.
  6. Go to the Configuration tab.
  7. A display view can be seen in the Configuration tab.
  8. Click Front module value.
  9. Expand the drop-down list in the Front module value.
    • Click AQR2530, front module, empty to see an empty display.
    • Click AQR2532, temperature to see the temperature symbol in the display.
    • Click AQR2535, temp., rel. humidity to see the temperature and relative humidity symbol in the display.
  10. The corresponding temperature and humidity values will reflect below the Front module value.
