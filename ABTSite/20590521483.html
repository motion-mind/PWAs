Duplicating device

In the Room segment editor you can duplicate a device.

Duplicate a device

  1. In the Room segment editor, select the lighting device or application function you want to duplicate.
  2. Right-click your selection and select Duplicate from the context menu.
  1. The selected item is duplicated and displayed at the end of the list.
