Adding the serial number to the QMX device (offline)

Add the serial number manually or use a barcode reader to be more efficient. The serial number and the barcode are available on the device.

  1. In the KNX PL-Link area, select the KNX PL-Link bus.
  2. Select the room in the table view.
  3. Select the Serial number column and enter the serial number of this device.
