Downloading a control program to a device

This procedure describes how to fully load the control program to the selected discovered device.

  1. A device is connected and discovered.
  1. Go to Startup > Configure and download.
  2. In the Engineered devices tab, right-click a device that matches a discovered device.
  3. Select Load and then Full (device may restart).
  4. You have downloaded a control program to a device.
  5. Switch off unused Ethernet ports.
