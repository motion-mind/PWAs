Engineering data status information

Shows engineering data status for selected device (the Enable engineering data on device check box is activated).

Settings > Device defaults

1) Site: Selected device in ABT Site (offline data)

3) Device: Selected device (online data)

2) Site: Activities of device, containing

  • Last modified date/time
  • Last date/time of full load
  • Last date/time of engineering data load

4) Device: Activities of selected online device, containing

  • Engineering Data Status (no data, outdated, valid)
  • Last modified date/time
  • Last date/time of full load
  • Last date/time of engineering data load
