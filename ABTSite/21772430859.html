[CenSsnCmp21] Central seasonal compensation 21, summer/winter compensation, with central outside air temperature

Configurator

Program blocks are stored in the library without I/Os. Use the auto-create and auto-connect functions to create I/Os and/or to connect them to the interface blocks, e.g., R_A, CMD_B. Alternatively, take the I/Os from the folder containing the predefined I/Os in the library and/or from the plant and connect them to the interface blocks.

Function

CenSsnCmp21 adjusts the room setpoints for comfort based on the characteristic curve and depending on the outside air temperature. It uses a group mechanism.

The most important functions in the program block are:

  • Calculation and distribution of outside temperature, depending on the compensated cooling setpoint for comfort based on the characteristic curve
  • Calculation and distribution of outside temperature, depending on the compensated heating setpoint for comfort based on the characteristic curve

Further information

CenSsnCmp21 evaluates the outside air temperature and calculates the Comfort temperature setpoints for heating (SpHCmfCmp) and cooling (SpCCmfCmp), and distributes the setpoint values to the group members.
