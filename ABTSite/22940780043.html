M-bus online engineering

You can go online with the M-bus editor. Components that go online are: Properties, Network tree, BACnet data points. When going online all engineering is disabled. Library workflows are disabled. All actions such as copy/duplicate/delete/create are disabled in all editors.

For M-bus online engineering make sure the related M-bus devices are connected to the network of the PXC4/5/7 device and powered up. The M-bus network and the connected M-bus devices were offline, created in the tool and loaded into the PXC4/5/7.
