Integrating Desigo CC in the BACnet/SC network

Desigo CC is integrated on the applicable floor hubs. A separate port must be set up in Desigo CC for each floor hub. This optimizes data traffic between Desigo CC and the floor hubs. Do not integrate the building hub as this would route all data traffic through the building hub and result in data overload.
