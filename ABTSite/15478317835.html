Assigning group members to group managers

Assign a group member to a group manager

  1. In the Project tree, open the Plant > Resources > Coordination folder and select a group manager.
    Plants navigation view
  2. Open the Properties tab.
  3. Open the Properties tab of the selected element.
  4. Click Group members.
  5. Do one of the following:
    • To assign specific group members, select the checkbox of a group member in the Assign column and click Assign selected.
    • To assign all group members, click Assign all.
  1. The group members are assigned to the group manager.
