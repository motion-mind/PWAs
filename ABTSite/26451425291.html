Main functions

Main components

  • Collect heating requests
  • Collect cooling requests
  • Control to the corresponding setpoints
  • One chiller with heat recovery/heat pump
  • Hot water storage tank with four sensors
  • Chilled water storage tank with four sensors
  • Main pump group, hot water side
  • Main pump group, chilled water side
  • Four shutoff valves for switching between the hot water storage tank and a recooling
  • Four shutoff valves for switching between the chilled water storage tank and a source
