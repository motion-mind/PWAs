Importing a BACnet/SC operational certificate from an external CA

Renew an external operational certificate

  1. You have an externally signed certificate.
    Exporting a BACnet/SC operational certificate signing request
  1. Open Building > Certificates management.
    Certificates management
  2. Select at least one device with external certificate type.
  3. Click Import external.
  4. Select the certificate file.
  5. Click Open.
  6. The operational certificate is renewed.
  7. The validity date of the operational certificate is extended.
  8. Do the following:
    • First time: Download the new operational certificate.
      Downloading a control program to a device
    • Second time: Download the new operational certificate with Only updated certificates.
      Downloading an updated certificate to a device

 

OR

 

Renew an external operational certificate from device details

  1. Open Building > Building structure.
  2. Select at least one device with external certificate type.
  3. Open the Details pane.
  4. Do the following:
  5. for the device version ≤ 1.5: select the BACnet/SC tab.
  6. for the device version ≥ 1.6: select the Certificates tab.
  7. Click Import certificate.
  8. Click Open.
  9. The operational certificate is renewed.
  10. The validity date of the operational certificate is extended.
  11. Do the following:
    • First time: Download the new operational certificate.
      Downloading a control program to a device
    • Second time: Download the new operational certificate with Only updated certificates.
      Downloading an updated certificate to a device
