Adapting the RDG2 device chart with the window/presence detector contact

  1. The window contact or/and presence detector are configured in the RDG2 device.
    Configuring a RDG2 window contact or/and presence detector
  1. In ABT Site, select a room.
  2. Right-click and select Show in program.
  3. Select a room, for example, [Room 101] > Resources > KNX PL-Link > [Room 101] > Device chart.
  4. The control program of the device chart opens.
  5. Drag the Window contact to the WndCont xFB.
  6. Drag the Presence detector to the PscDet xFB.
  7. The windows contact and presence detector are assigned and after downloading the control program visible on the RDG2 display.
