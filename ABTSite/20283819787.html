[ElPnl21x] Electrical panel (all options)

Name: [ElPnl21x] Electrical panel (all options)

Library hierarchy: Plants

ElPnl21x is a plant that contains electrical data points, alarm lamps, and an acknowledge button.

Instantiate ElPnl21x once in one automation station in the electrical cabinet.

Plant operating modes

  • None

Plant diagram

Main functions

Main components

  • Collect events and alarm indications from the plant SysFnctAs21x, from its own and from other automation stations.
  • Distribute acknowledge information to the plant SysFnctAs21x, to its own and to other automation stations.
  • Electrical data points
  • Electrical meter
