Function

Controls a VAV damper with external control.

Sends a relative setpoint (in %) for the air volume flow to the VAV damper.

External control (from the point of view of the PXC4/5/7): The VAV damper has its own control unit and the volume flow control of the control unit of the damper. It measures the volume flow and modulates a damper position to control the air volume flow.

The nominal air volume flow must be set (via analog configuration value). An analog calculated value shows the absolute setpoint (in m3/h) for the air volume flow.

This program block has the following optional functions:

Option: Air volume flow (1xAI)

Reads and displays the current air volume flow from the control unit of the VAV damper. A calculated value shows an air volume flow deviation (percentage of undersupply that indicates that the air volume flow cannot reach the setpoint even if the damper is fully open).

Option: Close command (1xBO)

Forces the VAV damper to close (and not to stay at minimum air volume flow when there is no setpoint, if it is configured in that way).

Option: Max. air volume flow command (1xBO)

Forces the VAV damper to go to the maximum air volume flow.

Option: Minimum air volume flow

Configuration for a minimal air volume flow (in m3/h) that is sent to the VAV damper.

Option: Position feedback (1xAI)

Reads the position of the damper.
