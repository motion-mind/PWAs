As per description:

Do the following to reset the device to factory state:

  1. Power off the device.
  2. Power on the device.
  3. Wait until all LEDs light up and turn off again, then press the Service button.
  4. Keep the Service button pressed until all LEDs light up, then release the button.
    All LEDs turn off, the device restarts.
  5. Wait until the device has fully started up – unconfigured (green RUN LED and red SVC LED are flashing).
