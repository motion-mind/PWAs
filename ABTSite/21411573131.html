Regenerate for ABT Site a BACnet/SC internal operational certificate

Every 3 to 5 years, the ABT Site operational certificate must be renewed. Basically, this should be done every time when the automation station certificate is renewed.

  1. Go to Settings.
  2. Open the Certificates task.
  3. Select the BACnet/SC certificates tab.
  4. In the Details view, click Regenerate internal certificate.
  5. The Confirm dialog box appears. Be aware, that this operation cannot be undone.
  6. Click OK.
  1. A new ABT Site operational certificate is created and in use.
