Extracting the ABT Installer setup package

You can choose to Install ABT Site and ABT Pro by using an Online or Offline ABT Installer setup package according to your requirements. The installation procedure remains the same for offline and online setups.

Note

  • The Online ABT Installer is distributed over SIOS pages via Internet.
  • Your Siemens contact partner exclusively distributes the Offline ABT Installer.
  • The product scope of Online ABT Installer and Offline ABT Installer is different. For more Information, contact your nearest Siemens office.
