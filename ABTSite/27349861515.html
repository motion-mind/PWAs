Replacing QMX3 hardware with QMX6 hardware

QMX6 devices can replace QMX3 devices without changing the software or wiring. Use the standard engineering workflow for QMX3 devices and use instead QMX6 devices on premise.

The following room devices are supported:

QMX3.P30 --> QMX6.P30

QMX3.P34 --> QMX6.P34

QMX3.P40 --> QMX6.P40

QMX3.P44 --> QMX6.P44

QMX3.P70 --> QMX6.P70

QMX3.P74 --> QMX6.P74

The QMX6 series is scheduled to replace QMX3 devices and is 100% backwards compatible.

The following hardware variants cannot be replaced with a new QMX6 variant at all:
- QMX3.P02
- QMX3.P36
- QMX3.P37
