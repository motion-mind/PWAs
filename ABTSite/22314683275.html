PXC network ports

TCP and UDP services can implement multiple applications. The port numbers are assigned to one of three categories by the IANA (www.iana.org)

  • Well-Known ports (0…1023)
    Reserved for specific applications and cannot be used by other applications. Examples: HTTP (80), SNMP (161)
  • Registered ports (1024…49151)
    Reserved for specific applications and cannot be used by other applications. Example: BACnet (47808)
  • Dynamic/private ports (49152…65535)
    Not predefined and can be used as needed.
